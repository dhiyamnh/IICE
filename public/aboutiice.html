Institute of Informatics and Computing in Energy (IICE)

Established in December 2016 as part of Universiti Tenaga Nasional’s BOLD2025 strategic plan, IICE is a multidisciplinary research and innovation hub. We drive advancements in sustainable energy, digital innovation, and applied computing through impactful research, industry collaboration, and knowledge exchange.

IICE is committed to bridging academic expertise with industry needs, nurturing talent, and creating solutions that contribute to Malaysia’s energy transformation and digital future.
