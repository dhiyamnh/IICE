// About IICE

Driving Energy Informatics for a Sustainable Future

The Institute of Informatics and Computing in Energy (IICE) was established in December 2016 under UNITEN’s BOLD2025 strategic plan. IICE is dedicated to advancing Energy Informatics — integrating ICT with energy systems to tackle challenges in efficiency, digitalisation, and sustainability.

Our research spans Smart Energy Systems, Energy Data Governance, and Energy Data Analytics & Visualisation, strengthening collaboration between academia, industry, and government.

// Our Research

Our Research Thrust Areas

IICE research focuses on three key thrusts that drive innovation in energy informatics, digitalisation, and visualisation.

ISES
Informatics for Smart Energy System: advancing ICT solutions for efficient and sustainable energy systems.
EDIG
Energy Data Infostructure & Governance: ensuring secure, reliable, and well-managed energy data infrastructures.
EDAV
Energy Data Analytics & Visualisation: harnessing big data and analytics to empower decision-making.
