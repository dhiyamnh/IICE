Our Research Focus Areas

The Institute of Informatics and Computing in Energy (IICE) focuses on cutting-edge research in informatics and computing applications for the energy sector. Our research areas span across various domains, addressing critical challenges and opportunities in the energy industry.

Intelligent and Secure Energy Systems (ISES)

Exploring advanced AI, machine learning, and cybersecurity to enhance energy system optimization, predictive maintenance, and reliable decision-making.

Energy Data Integration and Governance (EDIG)

Leveraging IoT, sensor networks, and cyber-physical systems to enable seamless data management, governance, and monitoring of smart energy infrastructures.

Energy Data Analytics and Visualisation (EDAV)

Applying computational modeling, simulation, and advanced visualization to support renewable integration, smart grids, and energy market forecasting.

Research Collaboration

IICE collaborates with industry partners, government agencies, and academic institutions to address real-world energy challenges. Our research contributes to sustainable energy development, energy security, and the transition to a low-carbon economy.

We welcome research collaborations and partnerships. Please contact us to discuss potential opportunities.
